# Account Management & User Support - HR Department Knowledge Base

## About Account Management
This knowledge base contains information about managing your account, team members, billing, subscriptions, and user-related inquiries for our SaaS platform.

---

## Section 1: Account Setup & Onboarding

### 1.1 Creating Your Account
- **Sign Up Process:** Visit our website and click "Sign Up" to create your account
- **Email Verification:** You'll receive a verification email within 5 minutes. Click the link to verify your email address
- **Initial Setup:** Complete your profile setup including company name, industry, and team size
- **Trial Period:** New accounts include a 14-day free trial with full access to all features

### 1.2 Account Types
- **Individual Plan:** For single users, includes basic features and 5GB storage
- **Team Plan:** For small teams (2-10 users), includes collaboration features and 50GB storage
- **Business Plan:** For larger organizations (11-50 users), includes advanced features, 500GB storage, and priority support
- **Enterprise Plan:** Custom pricing for 50+ users, includes dedicated support, custom integrations, and unlimited storage

### 1.3 Onboarding Resources
- **Welcome Email:** Check your inbox for a welcome email with getting started guides
- **Video Tutorials:** Access our video library from the Help menu in your dashboard
- **Setup Wizard:** Complete the interactive setup wizard to configure your account
- **Support:** Contact our support team at support@jupiteriq.com for personalized onboarding assistance
- **How to Add Team Members:** Go to Settings > Team Members and click "Invite Member". Enter their email address and assign a role. They'll receive an invitation email to join your team

## Section 2: User Management & Team Settings

### 2.1 Adding Team Members
- **Invite Users:** Go to Settings > Team Members and click "Invite Member"
- **Email Invitations:** Team members receive an email invitation with a secure link to join
- **Role Assignment:** Assign roles (Admin, Manager, Member, Viewer) when inviting users
- **Pending Invitations:** View and resend invitations from the Team Members page

### 2.2 User Roles & Permissions
- **Admin:** Full access to all features, billing, and account settings. Can delete account, transfer ownership, manage all users
- **Manager:** Can manage team members (add/remove), create and edit content, view analytics, export data, manage projects
- **Member:** Can create and edit content, view team members, limited access to settings (cannot manage billing or users)
- **Viewer:** Read-only access, cannot make changes, cannot export data, cannot view sensitive information
- **Custom Roles:** Enterprise plans can create custom roles with granular permissions (50+ permission settings available)
- **Role Hierarchy:** Admins can assign any role. Managers can assign Member/Viewer. Members cannot assign roles
- **Permission Overrides:** Enterprise admins can create permission exceptions for specific users or projects
- **Available Roles:** We offer four default roles: Admin (full access), Manager (team management), Member (content creation), and Viewer (read-only). Enterprise plans can create custom roles

### 2.3 Managing User Accounts
- **Edit User Details:** Admins can edit user names, emails, and roles from the Team Members page
- **Deactivate Users:** Temporarily disable access without deleting user data
- **Remove Users:** Permanently remove users from your team (data can be exported first)
- **Transfer Ownership:** Admins can transfer account ownership to another team member

### 2.4 Profile Management
- **Update Profile:** Edit your name, email, profile picture, and preferences from Account Settings
- **Password Changes:** Change your password from Security settings (requires current password)
- **Two-Factor Authentication:** Enable 2FA for enhanced security from Security settings
- **Notification Preferences:** Customize email and in-app notifications from Preferences

## Section 3: Billing & Subscriptions

### 3.1 Subscription Plans
- **Monthly Billing:** Pay monthly with the flexibility to cancel anytime
- **Annual Billing:** Save 20% with annual billing (billed once per year)
- **Enterprise Billing:** Custom billing terms available for Enterprise customers

### 3.2 Payment Methods
- **Credit Cards:** We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover
- **Payment Processing:** All payments are processed securely through Stripe
- **Billing Address:** Update your billing address from Billing > Payment Methods
- **Invoice Requirements:** Enterprise customers can request invoices for purchase orders

### 3.3 Managing Your Subscription
- **Upgrade/Downgrade:** Change your plan anytime from Billing > Subscription. Changes are prorated based on your billing cycle
- **Prorated Charges:** Plan changes are prorated based on your billing cycle
- **Add-Ons:** Purchase additional storage, users, or features from the Add-Ons section
- **Usage Limits:** Monitor your usage (storage, API calls, etc.) from the Usage dashboard
- **Changing Plans:** You can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime from Billing > Subscription. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period

### 3.4 Billing & Invoices
- **Billing Cycle:** Your billing date is shown in Billing > Subscription
- **View Invoices:** Download past invoices from Billing > Invoices
- **Payment History:** View all transactions from Billing > Payment History
- **Failed Payments:** We'll notify you via email if a payment fails. Update your payment method to avoid service interruption
- **Updating Payment Method:** Go to Billing > Payment Methods and click "Update Payment Method". Enter your new card details. The new payment method will be used for future charges

### 3.5 Cancellation & Refunds
- **Cancel Subscription:** Cancel anytime from Billing > Subscription (no cancellation fees)
- **Cancellation Timing:** Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period. Your subscription remains active until the end of your current billing period. You'll retain full access during this time. After cancellation, your account will be downgraded to a free plan (if available) or deactivated
- **Data Export:** Export your data before cancellation from Settings > Data Export
- **Refund Policy:** Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase for annual plans. Monthly plans are non-refundable but unused time remains accessible
- **Account Deletion:** Request permanent account deletion from Settings > Delete Account (30-day grace period for recovery)
- **Cancellation Reasons:** We collect optional feedback during cancellation to improve our service
- **Reactivation:** Cancelled accounts can be reactivated within 90 days with all data intact
- **Partial Refunds:** Prorated refunds available for annual plans cancelled mid-cycle (minus processing fees)

## Section 6: Account Limits & Usage

### 6.1 Storage Limits
- **Individual Plan:** 5GB storage
- **Team Plan:** 50GB storage
- **Business Plan:** 500GB storage
- **Enterprise Plan:** Unlimited storage
- **Storage Overages:** If you exceed your plan limit, you'll be notified and can either upgrade or purchase additional storage blocks (100GB increments at $10/month)
- **Storage Calculation:** Includes all files, attachments, exports, and cached data. Deleted items count toward storage for 30 days before permanent deletion

### 6.2 User Limits
- **Individual Plan:** 1 user
- **Team Plan:** Up to 10 users (can add 11th user for $15/month overage fee)
- **Business Plan:** Up to 50 users (can add additional users for $12/month each)
- **Enterprise Plan:** Unlimited users
- **Guest Users:** Enterprise plans can add unlimited guest users with limited permissions (no additional cost)
- **Inactive Users:** Users inactive for 90+ days don't count toward active user limits but retain access

### 6.3 API Rate Limits
- **Individual Plan:** 1,000 API calls per day (burst limit: 100 calls per minute)
- **Team Plan:** 10,000 API calls per day (burst limit: 500 calls per minute)
- **Business Plan:** 100,000 API calls per day (burst limit: 2,000 calls per minute)
- **Enterprise Plan:** Custom limits based on agreement (typically 1M+ calls/day)
- **Rate Limit Headers:** All API responses include `X-RateLimit-Limit`, `X-RateLimit-Remaining`, and `X-RateLimit-Reset` headers
- **Rate Limit Exceeded:** Returns HTTP 429 with Retry-After header. Implement exponential backoff

### 6.4 Monitoring Usage
- **Usage Dashboard:** View your current usage from Settings > Usage (real-time updates)
- **Usage Alerts:** Set up email alerts at 75%, 90%, and 100% of limits
- **Usage Reports:** Download monthly usage reports in CSV format
- **Historical Data:** View usage trends for the past 12 months
- **Upgrade Prompts:** In-app notifications when approaching limits with one-click upgrade options

### 6.5 Feature Limits by Plan
- **Individual Plan:** 5 projects, 10 workflows, basic reporting
- **Team Plan:** 25 projects, 50 workflows, advanced reporting, custom fields
- **Business Plan:** Unlimited projects/workflows, custom dashboards, advanced analytics, SSO
- **Enterprise Plan:** All features plus custom integrations, dedicated infrastructure, SLA guarantees

## Section 7: Account Transfers & Mergers

### 7.1 Account Ownership Transfer
- **Transfer Process:** Current admin initiates transfer from Settings > Account > Transfer Ownership
- **Requirements:** Both parties must have active accounts and confirm via email
- **Data Transfer:** All data, settings, and billing responsibility transfers to new owner
- **Timeline:** Transfer completes within 24 hours after both parties confirm
- **Billing Impact:** Billing responsibility transfers immediately. Prorated charges apply

### 7.2 Merging Accounts
- **Enterprise Feature:** Account merging available for Enterprise customers only
- **Process:** Contact enterprise-support@jupiteriq.com to initiate merge
- **Data Consolidation:** All users, projects, and data from source account merge into target
- **Duplicate Handling:** Duplicate users are merged (email matching). Duplicate projects can be renamed or archived
- **Timeline:** Merge process takes 3-5 business days with data validation

### 7.3 Account Splitting
- **Enterprise Feature:** Split large accounts into separate accounts
- **Use Cases:** Divestitures, department separation, subsidiary creation
- **Process:** Requires account admin approval and enterprise support coordination
- **Data Allocation:** Specify which users, projects, and data go to each new account
- **Timeline:** 5-7 business days with detailed migration plan

### 7.4 Bulk User Operations
- **CSV Import:** Upload CSV file to add multiple users at once (Business+ plans)
- **Bulk Role Changes:** Select multiple users and change roles simultaneously
- **Bulk Deactivation:** Deactivate multiple users at once (data preserved for 90 days)
- **Export User List:** Export all users with roles and status to CSV

## Section 8: Account Security & Access Management

### 8.1 Single Sign-On (SSO)
- **SSO Support:** SSO available for Business and Enterprise plans using SAML 2.0
- **Supported Providers:** Works with Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace, OneLogin, and other SAML providers
- **Setup Process:** Configure SSO from Settings > Security > Single Sign-On. Provide your IdP metadata URL
- **User Provisioning:** Automatic user provisioning and deprovisioning via SCIM (Enterprise feature)
- **Fallback Login:** Password-based login remains available as fallback option

### 8.2 Session Management
- **Session Duration:** Default session timeout is 30 days of inactivity
- **Active Sessions:** View all active sessions from Security > Active Sessions
- **Session Revocation:** Revoke individual sessions or all sessions except current
- **Device Management:** View and manage trusted devices from Security settings
- **Remote Logout:** Log out from all devices remotely if account is compromised

### 8.3 Password Policies
- **Password Requirements:** Minimum 8 characters, must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
- **Password History:** Cannot reuse last 5 passwords
- **Password Expiration:** Passwords expire after 90 days (configurable for Enterprise)
- **Password Reset:** Self-service password reset via email link (valid for 1 hour)
- **Admin Password Reset:** Admins can reset passwords for team members

### 8.4 API Key Management
- **Key Generation:** Generate API keys from Settings > Security > API Keys
- **Key Permissions:** Restrict API keys to specific endpoints and operations
- **Key Expiration:** Set expiration dates for API keys (recommended: 90 days)
- **Key Rotation:** Regularly rotate API keys for security (every 90 days recommended)
- **Key Revocation:** Revoke compromised or unused API keys immediately

### 8.5 Audit Logs & Activity Tracking
- **Activity Logs:** View account activity logs from Settings > Security > Activity Logs
- **Login History:** Track all login attempts with IP addresses and timestamps
- **Action Logs:** Log all account changes, user management actions, and settings modifications
- **Export Logs:** Export audit logs for compliance purposes (Enterprise feature)
- **Retention:** Audit logs retained for 90 days (1 year for Enterprise plans)
