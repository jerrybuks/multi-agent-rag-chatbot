## Section 2: Billing & Payment Methods

### 2.1 Accepted Payment Methods
- **Credit Cards:** Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover (all major card types)
- **Debit Cards:** Accepted if processed as credit (Visa/MasterCard debit)
- **Payment Processing:** All card payments processed securely through Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1)
- **Card Requirements:** Cards must have valid expiration date and CVV
- **International Cards:** International cards accepted (may incur foreign transaction fees from card issuer)

### 2.2 Alternative Payment Methods
- **ACH/Bank Transfer:** Available for Enterprise customers (US banks only, net 30 terms)
- **Wire Transfer:** Available for Enterprise customers (typically for annual payments)
- **Purchase Orders:** Accepted for Enterprise customers with approved credit
- **Invoicing:** Enterprise customers can request invoices for purchase orders
- **Payment Terms:** Standard terms are payment due on receipt. Net 30 available for Enterprise

### 2.3 Billing Cycles & Dates
- **Monthly Billing:** Billed on the same date each month (based on signup date)
- **Annual Billing:** Billed once per year on anniversary date
- **Proration:** Plan changes prorated based on remaining billing period
- **Billing Date Changes:** Billing dates can be adjusted for Enterprise customers
- **Weekend/Holiday Billing:** If billing date falls on weekend/holiday, charged on next business day

### 2.4 Payment Processing
- **Immediate Processing:** Card payments processed immediately upon charge
- **Authorization Holds:** Temporary authorization holds may appear before final charge
- **Processing Time:** Card payments typically post within 1-2 business days
- **Failed Payments:** Failed payments retried automatically (3 attempts over 7 days)
- **Payment Notifications:** Email notifications sent for successful and failed payments

### 2.5 Managing Payment Methods
- **Adding Cards:** Add payment methods from Billing > Payment Methods
- **Default Payment:** Set default payment method for automatic charges
- **Updating Cards:** Update card details (expiration, CVV) from payment methods page
- **Removing Cards:** Remove payment methods (must have at least one active method)
- **Card Verification:** New cards may require verification (small charge that's immediately refunded)

## Section 4: Payment Issues & Failed Payments

### 4.1 Failed Payment Handling
- **Automatic Retries:** Failed payments automatically retried 3 times over 7 days
- **Notification:** Email notifications sent for each failed payment attempt
- **Account Status:** Accounts remain active during retry period (typically 7 days)
- **Service Suspension:** Service may be suspended after retry period if payment fails
- **Reactivation:** Service reactivated immediately upon successful payment

### 4.2 Common Payment Failure Reasons
- **Insufficient Funds:** Card declined due to insufficient funds
- **Expired Card:** Card expired or expiration date incorrect
- **Card Limit:** Card reached spending limit
- **Bank Decline:** Bank declined transaction (fraud protection, etc.)
- **Invalid Card:** Card number, CVV, or billing address incorrect

### 4.3 Resolving Payment Issues
- **Update Payment Method:** Update card details from Billing > Payment Methods
- **Contact Bank:** Contact card issuer if payment declined (may need authorization)
- **Alternative Payment:** Add alternative payment method as backup
- **Payment Support:** Contact billing@jupiteriq.com for payment assistance
- **Manual Payment:** Enterprise customers can arrange manual payment (wire, ACH)

### 4.4 Payment Holds & Disputes
- **Chargebacks:** Chargebacks handled per card network rules
- **Dispute Resolution:** We work with customers to resolve payment disputes
- **Refund Requests:** Refund requests processed within 5-7 business days
- **Payment Holds:** Temporary holds may be placed during dispute resolution
- **Account Impact:** Chargebacks may result in account suspension until resolved

### 4.5 Payment Security
- **PCI Compliance:** All payment processing PCI DSS compliant
- **Encryption:** Payment data encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Tokenization:** Card numbers tokenized (we don't store full card numbers)
- **Fraud Prevention:** Fraud detection and prevention measures in place
- **Secure Storage:** Payment data stored securely with industry-standard security

## Section 5: Refunds & Credits

### 5.1 Refund Policy
- **Annual Plans:** Full refunds available within 30 days of purchase
- **Monthly Plans:** Monthly plans non-refundable but unused time remains accessible
- **Prorated Refunds:** Prorated refunds for annual plans cancelled mid-cycle (minus processing fees)
- **Processing Fees:** Processing fees (typically 2.9% + $0.30) deducted from refunds
- **Refund Timeline:** Refunds processed within 5-7 business days, appear on statement in 7-14 days

### 5.2 Refund Eligibility
- **Cancellation Timing:** Refunds available if cancelled within refund window
- **Service Issues:** Refunds available for significant service issues or outages
- **Billing Errors:** Refunds provided for billing errors on our part
- **Duplicate Charges:** Duplicate charges refunded immediately upon verification
- **Non-Refundable:** Setup fees, one-time charges typically non-refundable

### 5.3 Credit Issuance
- **SLA Credits:** Service credits issued for SLA violations (typically 10% of monthly fee per 0.1% below threshold)
- **Promotional Credits:** Promotional credits applied to account (non-refundable)
- **Goodwill Credits:** Goodwill credits issued for service issues (case-by-case basis)
- **Credit Application:** Credits applied to next invoice or refunded if account closed
- **Credit Expiration:** Credits typically don't expire but may have expiration dates for promotional credits

### 5.4 Refund Process
- **Refund Request:** Submit refund requests to billing@jupiteriq.com with account details
- **Review Process:** Refund requests reviewed within 2 business days
- **Approval:** Approved refunds processed within 5-7 business days
- **Refund Method:** Refunds issued to original payment method
- **Confirmation:** Email confirmation sent when refund processed

### 5.5 Partial Refunds & Proration
- **Prorated Refunds:** Refunds prorated based on unused service period
- **Usage Calculation:** Usage calculated from cancellation date to end of billing period
- **Add-On Refunds:** Add-ons refunded if cancelled within refund window
- **Annual to Monthly:** Downgrades from annual to monthly refunded prorated difference
- **Processing Fees:** Processing fees may be deducted from refunds per policy

## Section 9: Payment Processing & Security

### 9.1 Payment Security Standards
- **PCI DSS Compliance:** All payment processing complies with PCI DSS Level 1 standards
- **Tokenization:** Credit card numbers are tokenized and never stored in full
- **Encryption:** All payment data encrypted using industry-standard encryption (AES-256)
- **Secure Transmission:** All payment transactions use TLS 1.2+ encryption
- **Regular Audits:** Payment systems undergo regular security audits and penetration testing

### 9.2 Fraud Prevention
- **Fraud Detection:** Automated fraud detection systems monitor all transactions
- **Risk Scoring:** Transactions are scored for fraud risk before processing
- **Manual Review:** High-risk transactions flagged for manual review
- **Account Verification:** Additional verification may be required for large or unusual transactions
- **Chargeback Prevention:** Proactive measures to prevent chargebacks and disputes

### 9.3 Payment Processing Partners
- **Primary Processor:** Stripe serves as our primary payment processor (PCI DSS Level 1)
- **Backup Processors:** Backup payment processing capabilities for redundancy
- **International Processing:** Support for international payment methods and currencies
- **Processing Fees:** Standard processing fees apply (2.9% + $0.30 for cards, varies by method)
- **Settlement Times:** Funds typically settle within 2-3 business days

### 9.4 Payment Method Security
- **Card Storage:** Only last 4 digits and expiration dates stored for display
- **CVV Handling:** CVV codes never stored after initial transaction
- **Card Updates:** Customers must re-enter full card details when updating payment methods
- **Expired Cards:** Automatic notifications sent before card expiration
- **Multiple Cards:** Customers can store multiple payment methods with one set as default

### 9.5 Payment Disputes & Chargebacks
- **Dispute Process:** Disputes handled through payment processor's dispute resolution system
- **Response Time:** We respond to disputes within required timeframes (typically 7-10 days)
- **Documentation:** Comprehensive documentation provided to support dispute resolution
- **Chargeback Fees:** Chargeback fees may apply (typically $15-25 per chargeback)
- **Prevention:** We work proactively to prevent disputes through clear communication and service quality
