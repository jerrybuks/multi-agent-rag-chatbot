- **Content License:** By uploading content, customers grant us a license to store, process, and display it as necessary to provide the service

### 1.4 Account Termination

## Section 2: Privacy & Data Protection

### 2.1 Data Collection & Use
- **Data We Collect:** Account information, usage data, payment information, and content you upload
- **How We Use Data:** To provide and improve our service, process payments, send notifications, and comply with legal obligations
- **Data Sharing:** We don't sell customer data. We share data only with service providers necessary to operate the service (hosting, payment processing)
- **Marketing Communications:** Customers can opt out of marketing emails while retaining essential service communications
- **Analytics:** We use aggregated, anonymized data for analytics and service improvement

### 2.2 GDPR Compliance
- **Data Subject Rights:** Customers have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing
- **Data Processing Basis:** We process data based on contract performance, legitimate interests, and consent
- **Data Protection Officer:** Contact dpo@jupiteriq.com for GDPR-related inquiries
- **Data Breach Notification:** We'll notify affected customers within 72 hours of discovering a data breach
- **Data Transfer:** Data may be transferred outside the EU with appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses)

### 2.3 CCPA Compliance (California)
- **California Privacy Rights:** California residents have rights to know, delete, and opt-out of sale (we don't sell data)
- **Non-Discrimination:** We won't discriminate against customers who exercise their privacy rights
- **Verification:** We may require identity verification for certain privacy requests
- **Authorized Agents:** California residents can designate authorized agents to make privacy requests
- **Contact:** Submit CCPA requests to privacy@jupiteriq.com

### 2.4 Data Security Measures
- **Encryption:** All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access controls and multi-factor authentication
- **Security Audits:** Regular security audits and penetration testing
- **Incident Response:** 24/7 security monitoring and incident response procedures
- **Employee Training:** Regular security and privacy training for all employees

### 2.5 Data Retention & Deletion
- **Active Accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data deleted 30 days after account deletion (90 days for Enterprise)
- **Backup Retention:** Backups retained for 30 days, then permanently deleted
- **Legal Holds:** Data may be retained longer if subject to legal hold or investigation
- **Right to Deletion:** Customers can request data deletion per GDPR/CCPA rights

### 4.1 Service Level Agreements (SLAs)
- **Uptime SLA:** 99.9% uptime guarantee for Business and Enterprise plans

- **Payment Terms:** Standard terms are net 30, custom terms negotiable for Enterprise

### 4.5 Renewal & Cancellation Terms
- **Auto-Renewal:** Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled before renewal date
- **Renewal Notice:** Customers receive renewal notices 30 days before renewal
- **Cancellation Window:** Cancellations can be made anytime, effective at end of billing period
